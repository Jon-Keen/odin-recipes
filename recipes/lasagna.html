Brenda's Godly Lasagna

Have you ever wanted a fast and cheap lasagna? Well look no further than Brenda's godly lasagna.

With a total prep and cook time of 1 hour you can feed those hungry friends in a flash!

Ingredients

  • 1 - 16oz package lasagna noodles
  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 - 16oz jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
